# Hardware Enhancement Brainstorming for FluxTune

## Physical User Interface
- Rotary encoders with push/long-press for intuitive navigation
- Multi-color LED indicators for station type or signal strength
- Tactile feedback (vibration motor) for tuning/mode changes

## Audio Output Improvements
- Small speaker with amplifier for better sound
- Analog filtering for authentic radio hiss/band noise
- Headphone jack with auto-mute for speaker

## Power and Portability
- Battery operation (LiPo + charging circuit)
- Low-power sleep modes
- Power-on LED or battery level indicator

## Antenna and RF Simulation
- Telescoping/external antenna for realism
- Simulate antenna effects in software (e.g., unplugged mode)

## Physical Case and Ergonomics
- Custom 3D-printed or laser-cut enclosure
- Retro styling, labels, dials

## Expansion Ports
- I2C/SPI headers for future add-ons
- Serial debug port

---

## Panel LED Ideas
- One LED as a simple "power on" indicator
- Second LED as a meaningful/interesting indicator:
  - **Signal Activity:** Blinks or pulses with received signal strength
  - **Station Lock:** Lights up when tuned to a valid station
  - **Mode Indicator:** Color or blink pattern changes with mode (CW, Numbers, Pager, RTTY)
  - **Message Traffic:** Flashes when a station is actively transmitting
  - **Band Activity:** Shows band is "busy" (multiple stations active)
  - **Error/Alert:** Blinks on error, low battery, or special event
